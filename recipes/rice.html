Princess Mononoke's Rice Porridge

Description

A delicious warm rice porridge, a great breakfast or lunch in a cold winter day.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white Rice
  • 6 cups water
  • 3 teaspoons dashi miso paste
  • Shoyu
  • 4 eggs
  • Diced basil
  • Hot Chili & Seaseme oil

Steps

  1. Add both the water and rice to a pot over high heat. Once boiling, cover the pot and put the heat on low. Let it simmer for about 40 minutes until it turns soft and porridge-like. Add the miso paste and a splash of soy sauce (to taste) and mix over low heat until the miso paste is dissolved- about 5 minutes.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs until light and fluffy, then pour into the rice bowl. Stir to combine and take off the heat. After serving into 4 bowls garnish each with sliced basil and hot chili oil and/or sesame oil.
